## Bio

Since very young, Camilo Diaz has worked with several Salsa bands in Colombia, recording, producing and performing. At the end of the 90s, he joined forces with the most popular radio station in Colombia "Latina Stereo" and opened "Latina Stereo Studio" where he worked with the best and most talented musicians in Medellin, learning from the true maestros of Salsa themselves.

He moved to Australia in 2002 and to Thailand a couple of years later where he has been performing as a freelance percussionist and DJ in the most popular clubs and hotels all over Thailand. Indonesia, Vietnam and Pakistan have also witnessed the talent of this energetic and passionate artist.
